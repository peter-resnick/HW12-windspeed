Latitude vs Wind Speed



Wind Speed did not seem to have a very strong correlation to city latitudes. Generally, the highest winds were seen in the northern hemishpere, but not a significant amount more than in the south. Wind Speeds tended to stay lower throughout the cities examined, with an expectedly lower amount of cities experiencing very high (>20 mph) winds
